Progressive Events needs help to maintain locales

Progressive Events is built by the community. To be the resource we dream it could be, we need volunteers to take administrative roles for their local commmunity to ensure that events are added and maintained. While we expect this site to be a simple resource to those looking for events in their area, we also know it is critical to make sure there are things to look at. For this reason, Progressive Events is currently seeking geographically diverse members to serve as City Czars for their local community. The idea is to have an on-the-ground resource within the city/municipality/region that is dedicated to ensuring the population of community events is listed, not just a select sample. The role will also involve cleaning user-posted events and venues to ensure they are appropriately formatted and not duplicates, for instance.

“Hey, I could do that!”

That is exactly the attitude that is needed. We are looking for people who feel a responsibility to keep their community informed and aware of the political events in their area.

“How much time would this take?”

It of course depends upon your area and the level of citizen involvement. Major metropolitan areas will likely require more effort from the Czars than smaller municipalities. Regardless, events are being planned, posted, and updated (hopefully) every week, so the time commitment will be variable.

“What, explicitly, are the tasks?”

There are both tasks that are required and those that hopefully will be performed naturally as a side-effect of being part of the Progressive Events community. Obvious tasks include:

  • moderation of newly posted events
  • maintenance of critical local government events like city council meetings
  • removal of duplicate events
  • submission of events in your community

“Do I need coding skills to help?”

You don't need to code or program for this role. There is a simple web-based control panel that will let you modify events.

“I don't want to be a City Czar, how else can I contribute?”

Anyone can still contribute through the normal homepage by submitting events. Also be sure to engage in the events in your community and encourage others to engage as well.

“How do I apply?”

To apply, fill out an application and Progressive Events will get back to you.
